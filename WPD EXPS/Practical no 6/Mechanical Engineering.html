Department Of Mechanical Engineering

Department At A Glance

It is privilege to introduce our Mechanical department which is considered as one of the mother branches in Engineering. Since 2008 our dedicated and well qualified staff has been imparting quality education and as a result the intake of Mechanical department has grown from 60 to 240.

Here in the department we have CNC Production center, well equipped workshop and Advanced Labs which helps students to cut the gap between industry and academia.

Vision

"Imparting quality education, looking after overall development of students and conducting need based activities for successful career in Mechanical Engineering."

Mission

  • Develop the students for professionalism.
  • Prepare the students to take up career in different fields.
  • Imbibe moral and ethical values in students.

Program Educational Objectives

  • The student will be able to pursue successful career with strong domain knowledge.
  • The student will be able to continue professional development through self learning and higher education.
  • The student will be able to Seek lifelong learning through moral & ethical values.
